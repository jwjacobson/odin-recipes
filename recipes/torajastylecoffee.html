Toraja-style Coffee

Description

This is how to make coffee in the Toraja style.

Ideally you should use Toraja coffee, but any kind will do.

Ingredients

  • finely ground coffee
  • sugar
  • a small coffee glass, like the one pictured

Steps

  1. Heat water not quite to boiling
  2. While the water is heating, place 1 tsp cofee and 1 tsp sugar in your glass
  3. Once hot, pour water into glass and stir thoroughly with a spoon
  4. Let the grounds settle for a minute or so (optional: cover the glass so it stays hot)
  5. Sip the coffee without getting much of the grounds in your mouth
